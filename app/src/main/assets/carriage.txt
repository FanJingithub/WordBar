He sat in the corner of a second-class carriage.
他坐在一节二等车厢的角落里。
Her legs were long and fine, her hips slender, her carriage erect.
她长腿细臀，身姿笔直。
A carriage door struck him as a train drew into Basildon station.
当火车驶进巴斯尔登站时，一节车厢的门撞到他了。
I fought my way into a carriage just before the doors closed.
我刚挤进车厢，车门就关上了。
The conductor shambled to the next carriage.
售票员慵懒地拖着脚走向下一节车厢。
Davis went to regain his carriage.
戴维斯回到他的马车上。
The hired carriage was dismissed.
雇来的马车被打发走了。
I dismissed the carriage and walked the last mile. 
我把马车打发走了,徒步走完最后一英里路. 
The carriage upset and we were all spilled into the ditch. 
马车翻了,我们都摔到了沟里. 
 The carriage can post from London to Bath in only two days. 
这辆马车只要两天就能从伦敦赶到巴斯.
