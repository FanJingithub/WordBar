Buying a house can be a very tiresome business.
买房子会是件很麻烦的事。
He can be a very tiresome child at times. 
他有时候会是一个非常讨厌的孩子. 
His doubts and hesitations were tiresome. 
他的疑惑和犹豫令人厌烦. 
He spent his life in pointlessly tiresome drudgery. 
他的一生都在做毫无意义的烦人的苦差事. 
It would be too tiresome to wait in the queue.
排队等候太烦人。
These tiresome creatures eat holes in the leaves.
这些讨厌的虫子在叶子上咬出了洞。
Nothing is more tiresome than a superannuated pedagogue.
老朽无能的学究最令人讨厌不过。
He was tiresome in contending for the value of his own labors. 
他老为他自己劳动的价值而争强斗胜,令人生厌. 
She lingered, and resisted my persuasions to departure a tiresome while. 
她踌躇不去, 我好说歹说地劝她走,她就是不听. 
 It was tiresome and unfair and her brother was sick of it. 
这真叫人腻味,而且也不公平,她哥哥对此可是厌烦了.
